Willamette Superfund School:The Willamette is your river, and you have a say in how the Portland Harbor Superfund site should be cleaned up (the plan will be locked in on July 1 and this is our VERY brief chance to be heard)! Tour this reach of the river and learn where the Superfund sites are, who is involved in the cleanup, what caused the contamination, what are the cleanup options, and how you can help! Break Free - Anacortes

Here in the Pacific Northwest, we are converging on March Point in Anacortes, WA, whose refineries produce nearly half of all the gas and diesel consumed in our region, and where Shell Oil is proposing to add a new facility that would even increase crude oil train traffic on the Columbia River.This is a time to say No. Together, with one voice, all over the world. NO to fossil fuels. Yes to our common humanity. Yes to a sustainable human future. We will not sell out our future and our children’s future to our fossil fuel addiction. This is what BREAK FREE means.

You don’t have to get arrested. But if you know yourself to be a citizen of the world, and if you have a stake in the future, even just a sentimental one …. Please put in an appearance. Hosted by many organizations, including 350PDX.org.
